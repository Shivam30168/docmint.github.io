Privacy Policy for DocMint

Effective Date: January 14, 2026

1. Information Collection

DocMint does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. All your interactions with the app are private and remain on your device.

2. Document Data

All document data processed using DocMint is handled locally on your device. No document content is transmitted to or stored on any external servers.

3. Payments

If you make purchases through DocMint, payments are securely handled by Razorpay. DocMint does not store any payment information.

4. Data Storage

No user information, documents, or personal data are stored on any servers. Your data remains on your device unless you choose to share it externally.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and will become effective immediately upon posting.
